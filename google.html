Google Play

I had the opportunity to collaborate with Google Play's Creative Shop team as a visual designer and illustrator. Throughout my tenure at Google Play, my primary responsibility entailed crafting a diverse range of custom imagery and illustrations to be utilized worldwide on the Google Play store. The scope of my work encompassed Play's distinctive branding and illustrative style.

Below are examples of the work I have accomplished during my tenure at Play, showcasing a range of global campaigns and the extensive imagery I have contributed to Play's Evergreen image library. Google Play, the renowned online store, attracts billions of users who delight in their favorite app content, spanning movies, fitness, lifestyle, books, entertainment, and so much more.

As designers at Play, our main goal is to make the platform super awesome and helpful for users. We make sure to have eye-catching and informative images that bring joy and provide useful info to everyone.
